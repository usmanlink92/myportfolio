Employee Travel Allowance System

Employees travel across manufacturing sites, warehouses and offices across the country on business trip. Travel Expense, Employee Accommodation and Tickets booking system was developed in Oracle Application Framework (OAF/Java) along with Oracle Workflows and Databases. Employee can initiate a Travel Espense, manager can approve or update the travel details, administration can book for tickets and accommodation, and accounts department can cross check and verify the expense sheet if travel was done on personal expense.
